🏰 Game Guide

Selecting Units & Buildings

TapSelect a unit or building. Tap it again to deselect.
Drag from unitBox-select multiple units at once.
Tap buildingOpens its production panel in the bar at the bottom.

Giving Orders

Double-tap groundMove selected units to that location.
Double-tap enemyAttack that enemy unit or building.
Long-press enemyAttack order (alternative to double-tap).
⚔️ buttonSwitch to Attack mode — next tap targets an enemy.
🏃 buttonSwitch to Move mode — next tap is a move destination.

Camera

Drag empty groundPan the camera.
PinchZoom in / out.
Tap minimapJump camera to that map location.

Building & Production

Tap a buildingSee what it produces. Tap an item to queue it.
⚙️ buttonFull build menu — all structures, infantry, vehicles.
Place buildingAfter selecting from menu, tap the map to place it.

Selecting Units & Buildings

ClickSelect a unit or building.
Click + DragDraw a box to select multiple units at once.
Shift + ClickAdd or remove from current selection.
EscDeselect everything.
Click buildingOpens its production panel in the bottom bar.

Giving Orders

Right-click groundMove selected units to that location.
Right-click enemyAttack that unit or building.

Camera

Mouse to edgeScroll the camera in that direction.
Arrow keys / WASDScroll the camera.
Scroll wheelZoom in / out.
Click minimapJump camera to that location.

Building & Production

Click a buildingShows what it can produce in the bottom bar.
⚙️ buttonFull build menu — all structures, infantry, vehicles.
Place buildingClick to place after selecting from the menu. Right-click to cancel.

Objective

WinDestroy ALL of your opponent's buildings. You are BLUE, enemy is RED (or vice versa in multiplayer).

Economy

💰 CreditsSpent on all units and buildings. Earned by Ore Trucks mining yellow ore patches.
⛏️ RefineryOre Trucks deposit ore here. Building one spawns a bonus Ore Truck automatically.
⚡ PowerPower Plants generate power. Power usage (shown as ⚡gen/used) must stay positive or production pauses. More surplus = faster builds (up to 2×).

Buildings

🏭 Con. YardBuild all structures from here. Required first building.
⚡ Power PlantGenerates 100 power. Always build these early.
🏠 BarracksTrains infantry units (GI, Sniper, War Dog, Repairman).
🏗️ War FactoryBuilds vehicles (Ore Truck, Grizzly Tank, IFV).
🗼 TurretAuto-attacks nearby enemies. Strong base defense.
📡 RadarRequired to train Repairman.

Units

🪖 GIBasic infantry. Cheap and fast to train.
🎯 SniperLong range, high damage, fragile.
🐕 War DogVery fast, melee only.
🔧 RepairmanRepairs any friendly building to full HP. Requires Barracks + Radar.
🛡️ Grizzly TankHeavy armour, high damage. Main battle unit.
🚗 IFVFast attack vehicle. Good all-rounder.

Tips

⚡Build at least 2 Power Plants early — surplus power speeds up all production.
⛏️Build a second Refinery when you can afford it — doubles your income.
🗼Ring your base with Turrets before attacking. They have long range and high fire rate.
🔧Keep a Repairman near your base — one Repairman can keep Turrets alive indefinitely.
